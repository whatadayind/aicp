# AICP Roadmap

## Vision

Build an AI-powered Customer Engagement Platform that enables businesses to communicate with customers through email, automation, AI, and analytics.

---

- PostgreSQL

- Organizations (Multi-tenancy)
- Roles & Permissions
- User Management

# MVP Goal

The first public release should allow users to:

- Register
- Create an Organization
- Import Contacts
- Create Email Campaigns
- Schedule Campaigns
- Track Opens & Clicks
- Build Simple Automation
- Use AI to Generate Email Content

---

# Long-Term Goal

Build a scalable, production-ready SaaS platform capable of serving thousands of businesses with AI-powered customer engagement.
