# AI Customer Engagement Platform (AICP)

> A modern AI-powered customer engagement platform built with Laravel, React, PostgreSQL, Redis, and Docker.

## Vision

AICP is an open, scalable, multi-tenant SaaS platform that helps businesses engage customers through intelligent communication and marketing automation.

The long-term goal is to provide an alternative to platforms such as:

- Brevo
- Mailchimp
- ActiveCampaign
- Customer.io

while adding AI-powered customer engagement capabilities.
